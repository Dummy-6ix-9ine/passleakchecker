How It Works

This tool scans a collection of billions of leaked passwords to check if yours has been compromised:

  1. Enter the password you want to check in the input field.
  2. The tool hashes your password and cross-references it with our list of breached databases.
  3. The results will show how many times your password has been found in leaked databases.
